Privacy

The IAB job survey is carried out nationwide by the Economix Research & Consulting Institute on behalf of the Institute for Employment Research (IAB), the research institution of the Federal Employment Agency (BA). The legal basis for this is § 282 Abs. 5 SGB III. It is a written main survey and three possible telephone follow-up surveys. TM Quadrat GmbH acts as a service provider for the short follow-up surveys in the first three quarters of the following year. All participating institutes bear the responsibility under data protection law and work in accordance with the extensive legal provisions on data protection.

Origin of addresses

The following information is transmitted to the Economix survey institute: name and address of the company, company number, number of employees subject to social security contributions as of 31.12. of the previous year, industry identifiers and existing contact information of a contact person. The legal basis for this is Article 28 GDPR.

What happens to your data?

After you have returned the completed questionnaire to us, Economix will separate it from your address. This remains with Economix until the end of the investigation and is then deleted. Of course, this also applies if you answer the questionnaire online. The information provided by all companies is processed anonymously in a large data set in which it is combined with information that is already available at the Federal Employment Agency about the company. The merging takes place exclusively via an anonymous code number, i.e. without name and address.

This data set is then evaluated at the IAB for scientific purposes. In principle, the following applies: None of the scientists is able to recognize from which company which information originates, address information is not accessible to them. The presentation of all results is completely anonymous. This means that no one can see from the results what answers individual companies or administrations have given. This also applies to the short telephone repeat surveys in the first three quarters of the following year. The identification of individual companies or administrations is also not possible here.

Voluntary participation

Your participation is of course voluntary, and you have the right to withdraw your participation at any time. In the event of non-participation or revocation, you will not incur any disadvantages. The revocation does not affect the previous processing.

Your rights and our guarantees

You can assert the following rights with regard to your survey data at any time:

  • Right

  • Right to rectification

  • Right to restriction of processing

  • Right to erasure

In case of complaints, you can also contact the responsible data protection supervisory authority. The research institutes involved in this project guarantee you:

  • Your information will only be evaluated in anonymous form;

  • the address of your company will in no case be passed on to third parties;

  • the scientists involved in the collection and evaluation are bound by data secrecy.

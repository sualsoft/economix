Zur IAB Stellenerhebung 2022

Methodology

The IAB Job Survey has been carried out annually since 1989. It is aimed at a representative selection of companies and administrative offices in West and East Germany. The farms are selected at random every year in order to keep the burden on the individual farms low.

Participation

Participation is voluntary, but we hope for your support! Your answers are very important to us, because only if we receive enough information from companies on personnel policy and staffing can we draw well-founded scientific conclusions about the current and future dynamics on the labour market. Please support us in this.

Important information

Please answer the questionnaire for the company/administration addressed in the cover letter. By this we mean the local production, trade, service or administrative site, and NOT the entire company, the entire group or the higher-level administrative office. The holding is the same local unit as when registering for social security (social security company number).

Please fill out the questionnaire even if your company/administrative office does not have its own personnel administration. It depends on the personnel situation on site. If this is not possible for formal reasons, please forward the questionnaire to the responsible body. Or let us know. We are happy to take care of this for you.

As a temporary employment agency, please indicate the persons employed by you as your employees or employees subject to social security contributions (and NOT as temporary workers â€“ this only applies to companies in which these persons are employed).

Results

The survey results are highly relevant both for practice-oriented policy advice and for scientific research, because the IAB job survey is unique in Germany. The resulting studies have a major influence on the decisions in the labour market policy of the federal government, the federal states and in Europe. At regular intervals, the IAB publishes press releases, short reports and articles in order to make the results available to a broad public. A selection can be found at: https://www.iab.de/stellenerhebung.

Important terms

Level of requirements of the occupations

The occupation information is recorded according to the Classification of Occupations 2010. In addition to the exact job title, this also requires information on the level of requirements:

  1. Positions that usually do not require a formal vocational qualification or a maximum of one year of regular vocational training correspond to the level of "helper". This also includes civil servants in the simple service.

  2. Bodies that have in-depth specialist knowledge, which is usually acquired in the context of a 2- to 3-year vocational/technical school education, have the level of "specialist". This also includes civil servants in the middle service.

  3. Positions requiring complex activities, planning and control tasks, specialist knowledge and skills are assigned to the "Specialist" level. Usually, activities that require a master craftsman/technician training or a bachelor's degree, or civil servants in the higher service are included.

  4. Positions for highly specialized activities, e.g. in development, research or diagnosis, management and management tasks in large companies, which usually require at least 4 years of higher education (master's degree, diploma or state examination, etc.) or in which civil servants are employed in the higher service, correspond to the level "expert".

Employment Agency

This includes the local agencies of the Federal Employment Agency, the working groups between the Federal Agency and the municipalities (joint institutions, gE) and the municipal institutions for job placement (job center). Private employment agencies, on the other hand, are not one of them.

Employees

The number of employees includes all those working in your company: in addition to the employees and workers, also the owners/

-inside, the family workers, the marginally employed (e.g. mini-jobs) and the trainees. In the case of public authorities and state-owned enterprises, civil servants and judges must also be counted. There must be a direct employment contract with employees. Freelancers, employees on loan via temporary work, subsidised employment relationships (FAV) and one-euro jobs are not counted.

Notified body

A registered position includes unsubsidized employment relationships, which are registered by employers to the employment agencies and basic security institutions for placement (without seasonal jobs).

Temporary /temporary work

Temporary/temporary workers are hired by temporary employment companies and loaned to temporary employment companies for a limited time. They are therefore not employees or employees subject to social security contributions in the company in which they are employed for a limited period of time. An employment contract exists between the temporary employment agency and the borrowed person.

Privacy

The IAB job survey is carried out nationwide by the Economix Research & Consulting Institute on behalf of the Institute for Employment Research (IAB), the research institution of the Federal Employment Agency (BA). The legal basis for this is Â§ 282 Abs. 5 SGB III. It is a written main survey and three possible telephone follow-up surveys. TM Quadrat GmbH acts as a service provider for the short follow-up surveys in the first three quarters of the following year. All participating institutes bear the responsibility under data protection law and work in accordance with the extensive legal provisions on data protection.

Origin of addresses

The following information is transmitted to the Economix survey institute: name and address of the company, company number, number of employees subject to social security contributions as of 31.12. of the previous year, industry identifiers and existing contact information of a contact person. The legal basis for this is Article 28 GDPR.

What happens to your data?

After you have returned the completed questionnaire to us, Economix will separate it from your address. This remains with Economix until the end of the investigation and is then deleted. Of course, this also applies if you answer the questionnaire online. The information provided by all companies is processed anonymously in a large data set in which it is combined with information that is already available at the Federal Employment Agency about the company. The merging takes place exclusively via an anonymous code number, i.e. without name and address.

This data set is then evaluated at the IAB for scientific purposes. In principle, the following applies: None of the scientists is able to recognize from which company which information originates, address information is not accessible to them. The presentation of all results is completely anonymous. This means that no one can see from the results what answers individual companies or administrations have given. This also applies to the short telephone repeat surveys in the first three quarters of the following year. The identification of individual companies or administrations is also not possible here.

Voluntary participation

Your participation is of course voluntary, and you have the right to withdraw your participation at any time. In the event of non-participation or revocation, you will not incur any disadvantages. The revocation does not affect the previous processing.
